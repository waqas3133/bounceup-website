Application Guide

How to Use BounceUp

1

Wet Hair Thoroughly

Soak your hair and scalp completely with warm water. This opens the cuticle and allows ingredients to penetrate.

2

Apply & Massage

Pump shampoo into hands, apply to scalp first, then work through lengths. Massage in circular motions for 60 seconds.

3

Rinse Completely

Rinse thoroughly until water runs clear. Avoid direct contact with eyes.

4

Follow with Conditioner

For best results, follow with BounceUp Conditioner. Leave for 2 minutes then rinse.
